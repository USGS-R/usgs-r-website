Contact

How to sign up for a USGS R-User email list?

Join the USGS R-User email list. This list will be used for announcements such as training opportunities and package developments. Email: <krogers@usgs.gov>.

How to get R training?

There are many resources for R training. Please see our list of online R training resources. If you are interested in going further and attending an in-person R training, please contact us for an updated schedule of trainings near you. Email <gs-w_r_admin@usgs.gov>.

How to contribute to USGS-R?

USGS-R is an open community within the USGS. If you would like to contribute your code, improve existing USGS-R packages, or just have a question, please contact us via email <gs-w_r_admin@usgs.gov>.
